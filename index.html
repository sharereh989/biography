Women, Life, Freedom!
A revolution in which women raised their voice to stand against injustice. By cutting their braided hair they showed how firm they are standing against the patriarchal system. Inspired by this revolution I tried to create a look that simply shows women and all the courage that is growing in their spirit. Just like a beautiful Persian carpet, the more they get kicked, the more precious they will become.

In color psychology, pink’s color meaning revolves around femininity, playfulness, immaturity and unconditional love, whereas blue’s color meaning ties closely to the sea, sky, peace and masculinity. Here I have tried to combine both colors to create a vibrant harmony.

Natural is beautiful! Dramatic eyes, Kajal and almond-shaped eyes are loved in India. At the same time, it is not common to remove hair from the face and all should look natural! In this makeup I tried to create a smokie eye with a Kajal taste on her eyes and leave the lips simply red and fresh.

Old is gold! every time that I get to talk to my mom about Makeup, we end up talking about Yardley, Max Factor, Nivea and all old brands that were available during her young days. This is my mom and with this look I tried to create a simple 70's look, resembling olden days and olden beauties.
